# ebay-deals

1 Key Findings

Time Series Analysis: Most deals are posted in the evening with activity peaking around 23:00 ( graph shows 23)

Price and Discount Analysis: Most products are priced under $200, with most items having no discount, but a few items have high discounts up to 63% ( from final graph we find out that its almost 63)

Shipping Information Analysis:  "shipping info unavailable” is the most common while some items show “Free shipping”

Text Analysis on Product Titles: Popular keywords include Apple, Laptop, iPhone, Tablet (samsung and gimbal aren't there)

Price Difference Analysis: Most products have little or no difference between original and sale prices but some items have significant price reductions

Discount Analysis: The top 5 deals have discounts ranging from 8% to 63% with high discounts being rare but notable
